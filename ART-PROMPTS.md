## The house style (reuse in every prompt)
> *Minimal vintage perfumery / apothecary aesthetic. Warm ivory & parchment background (#fbf6ee), muted gold (#bd9a4e), soft sepia and charcoal ink. Hand‑drawn engraving / fine line‑art feel, subtle paper grain, lots of empty space, no clutter, flat or very soft lighting, elegant and understated, antique label energy. No text unless asked. No harsh neon, no 3D plastic look, no busy gradients.*

**Global Midjourney suffix you can append:** `--style raw --ar 1:1 --v 6` (change `--ar` per asset).

**Motif suggestions by family (pick one per note):**
- **Citrus** (bergamot, lemon, orange, grapefruit, mandarin, lime, yuzu): a single citrus fruit with one leaf, cross‑section optional.
- **Fruity** (apple, pear, peach, cassis, raspberry): one ripe fruit on a stem.
- **Floral** (rose, jasmine, neroli, tuberose, ylang, iris, geranium): a single bloom with two leaves.
- **Green/Herbal** (lavender, sage, rosemary, basil, mint, galbanum, violet leaf): a sprig or single stem.
- **Spice** (pepper, cardamom, cinnamon, clove, saffron, ginger, nutmeg): a small pile or pod of the spice.
- **Woody** (sandalwood, cedar, vetiver, patchouli, oud, guaiac): a wood shaving curl or a cross‑section log.
- **Resin/Amber** (frankincense, myrrh, benzoin, labdanum, ambroxan): a resin tear / droplet on bark.
- **Mossy/Leather/Smoky** (oakmoss, birch tar, cade, suede): moss tuft, or a curl of smoke.
- **Animalic/Musk** (ambrette, civet accord, musks): an abstract soft swirl / pod (keep tasteful, no animals).
- **Gourmand** (vanilla, tonka, cocoa, coffee, praline, honey): a vanilla pod, coffee bean, or honey dipper.
- **Aquatic/Aldehyde** (calone, floralozone, aldehydes): an abstract wave or a single dewdrop with radiating lines.

### Workflow tips
- Generate the **background, icon set, and one note grid first** — they set the palette everything else matches.
- Ask for **transparent PNG** where the generator allows (icons, badges, note glyphs).
- Export app icons at **192px, 512px, and a 512px maskable** (centered with margins) to replace the files in `icons/`.
- Keep the same 4–5 colours throughout: ivory `#fbf6ee`, deep brown `#3c2b22`, gold `#bd9a4e`, soft rose `#e0789a`, teal `#4fb0b8`.
